About

What is CatForge?

CatForge is Texas State University’s premier software development lab. Every semester, we place Computer Science, Communication Design, and Business Administration students onto development teams to create software projects from the ground up.

Team Structure

Teams consist of:

  • 6 Student Developers
  • 1 Student Designers
  • 1 Student Project Manager
  • 1 Industry Mentor

Symposium Night

At the end of the semester, each team presents their project to other CatForge projects teams, Texas State students and staff, and industry sponsors. These collectives vote on the winning project based on scope, execution, technical difficulty, and presentation, and 1st and 2nd place winning teams receive an award for their work.
